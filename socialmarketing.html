Types of Social Media Marketing Services:

Social media advertising:

Social media advertising is a type of digital marketing where businesses pay for ad campaigns to appear on digital platforms. The goal of these campaigns is to reach specific target audiences and promote brands, ultimately leading to increased sales. Social media advertising is an effective way for marketers and advertisers to connect with users on the platforms they use most frequently.

Social media brand management services:

Brand management is a deliberate process of creating and controlling a brand. This process involves a combination of activities such as defining the brand, positioning it, and consistently delivering its value to the customers. A clear and well-executed brand management plan can help establish customer loyalty to your business.

Advertising/Ads campaign:

An advertising campaign is a marketing strategy consisting of one or more advertisements aimed at specific target audiences. The primary objective of an ad campaign is to increase conversions. The success of an advertising campaign relies on the proper selection of channels, strategies, and tactics.

There are five critical steps to creating an advertising campaign strategy. These include: setting an objective, defining the audience, creating segments, delivering the right message, and selecting the communication medium.
